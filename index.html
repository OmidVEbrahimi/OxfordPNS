Welcome to OxPNS

The Oxford Psychological Networks School (OxPNS) is a comprehensive statistical workshop designed to provide participants with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills in psychological network analysis.

Network analysis has become an increasingly important approach in psychology, offering unique insights into the complex relationships between psychological variables. This workshop brings together leading experts in the field to share their knowledge and experience with participants from around the world.

Join us at the historic University of Oxford for an intensive learning experience that will enhance your research capabilities and open new avenues for understanding psychological phenomena through network perspectives.
